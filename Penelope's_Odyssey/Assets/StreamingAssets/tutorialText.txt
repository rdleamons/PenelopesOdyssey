Use the W, A, S, and D keys to move. (Press enter to continue . . .)
Use space to jump.
Click & hold the left mouse button to stop and sniff for objects that could lead to Penelope's owner.
Click & hold the right mouse button to sniff for food.
Watch your hunger meter -- don't let Penelope starve!
Press shift to sprint, but watch out! This will make your hunger decrease faster!
